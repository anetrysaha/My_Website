I Am
Tim Berners-Lee

I am the founder of www: World Wide Web

Hi!

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989.Sir Tim is the Director of the World Wide Web Consortium(W3C ), a Web standards organization founded in 1994 which develops interoperable technologies (specifications, guidelines, software, and tools) to the lead the Web to its full potencial. He is a Director of the World Wide Web Foundation which was launched in 2009 to coordinate efforts to further the potencial of the Web to benefit humanity.

In 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Soceity. He has been the recipeint of several international awards including the Japan Prize, the Prince of Asturais Foundation Prize, the Millennuim Technology Prize, the Germany's die Quadriga award. In 2004 he was knighted by H.M. Queen Elizabeth and in 2007 he was awared the award of Merit. In 2009 he was elected a foreign associate of the National Academy of Sciences.
